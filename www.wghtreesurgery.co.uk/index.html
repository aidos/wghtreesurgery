Health and Safety

We strictly adhere to industry guidelines to ensure the safety and well-being of both employees and members of the public. In addition to having the necessary NPTC certifications, our employees have First Aid training and wear PPE.

Risk Assessments are carried out prior to each job and Method Statements are provided for all commercial work sites. We hold Public and Employer’s Liability insurance for £10,000,000.

Sustainability

All our waste is recycled in a multitude of ways.

We send all our woodchip off for biomass as a sustainable energy source. Our cord wood is also sent for high quality biomass.

Timber is milled at our yard and onsite to ensure interesting and characterful timber lives on as furniture.

We also produce firewood and deliver locally.
